Non-metric multidimensional scaling (NMDS) analysis upon Yue & Clayton measure of dissimilarity between all the samples. Proximity of the points on the plot reflects similarities of bacterial communities structure. Vectors show the correlations (Pearson's coefficient) between the MOTUs and the community structure shifts between the samples. Only statistically significant correlations are shown.
